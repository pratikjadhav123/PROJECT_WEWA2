rna Fort is probably the most visited fort around Pune. It has gained this popularity due to it's historical importance and the challenging trek that it offers. Torna was the first fort captured by Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj in 1646. For nature lovers and mountaineers, this place is paradise. It is the highest fort in Pune and offers some spectacular views of the Sahyadri mountains. Most structures on the fort are still intact.
